# Introduction to Cybersecurity

## Overview
Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks, and data from cyber threats such as unauthorized access, attacks, and data breaches. It plays a critical role in ensuring the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of information.

## Why Cybersecurity Matters
With the increasing use of digital technologies, organizations and individuals are exposed to various cyber risks. Cybersecurity helps prevent:
- Data breaches
- System disruptions
- Financial and reputational damage

## Core Principles
The foundation of cybersecurity is based on the CIA Triad:
- **Confidentiality**: Ensuring that information is accessible only to authorized users.
- **Integrity**: Maintaining the accuracy and trustworthiness of data.
- **Availability**: Ensuring systems and data are accessible when needed.

## Common Cyber Threats
- Malware (viruses, worms, ransomware)
- Phishing and social engineering
- Network attacks
- Unauthorized access
